Welcome

Bangladesh was a province of Pakistan until 1971. After 1971 Bangladesh became the independent country. Sheikh Mujibur Rahman was the president. From the very beginning Sheikh Mujib tried to make Bangladesh as prosperous country. His birthplace is Gopalganj, a small district of Bangladesh. The people of Gopalganj had dream to make this town as ideal district for knowledge and technology. But after the assassination of Sheikh Mujibur Rahman,no ruler of Bangladesh took care of this district because it was the birthplace of Sheikh Mujibur Rahman.After long time his daughter Sheikh Hasina came to Bangladesh from London and following her father to make his dream a reality and the people of Gopalganj started new dream around Sheikh Hasina daughter of Sheikh Mujibur Rahman.Gopalganj is proud of such a prominent leader Sheikh Mujibur Rahman
